🍝 Gulab Jamun 🍝

Detail Information
Preparation Time 45 minutes
Difficulty Medium
Ingredients • 1 cup khoya
• 2 tbsp flour
• ¼ tsp baking powder
• 2–3 tbsp milk
• 2 cups sugar
• 2 cups water
• Cardamom, rose water
• Oil/ghee for frying
Instructions 1. Knead khoya with flour and milk.
2. Shape into balls.
3. Fry until golden.
4. Make sugar syrup with cardamom & rose water.
5. Soak jamuns in syrup.
6. Serve warm or chilled.
