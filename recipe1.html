🍝 Spaghetti Carbonara 🍝

Detail Information
Preparation Time 30 minutes
Difficulty Medium
Ingredients • 200g spaghetti
• 100g pancetta
• 2 eggs
• 50g parmesan cheese
• Salt & pepper
Instructions 1. Boil spaghetti until al dente.
2. Fry pancetta until crispy.
3. Mix eggs and parmesan.
4. Combine spaghetti, pancetta, and egg mixture.
5. Season with salt and pepper.
