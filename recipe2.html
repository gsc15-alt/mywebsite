🍝 Chocolate Cake 🍝

Detail Information
Preparation Time 50 minutes
Difficulty Easy
Ingredients • 1½ cups flour
• ½ cup cocoa powder
• 1 cup sugar
• 2 eggs
• 1 cup milk
• ½ cup oil
• Baking powder, soda, vanilla
• Chocolate frosting
Instructions 1. Preheat oven to 180°C.
2. Mix dry ingredients.
3. Add wet ingredients.
4. Stir in hot water.
5. Bake 30–35 minutes.
6. Cool and frost.
