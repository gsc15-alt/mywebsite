🍝 Paneer Butter Masala 🍝

Detail Information
Preparation Time 40 minutes
Difficulty Medium
Ingredients • 200g paneer
• 2 onions
• 3 tomatoes
• 2 tbsp butter
• 2 tbsp cream
• Spices (chili, turmeric, coriander, garam masala)
• Salt & coriander leaves
Instructions 1. Sauté onions in butter.
2. Add ginger-garlic paste.
3. Stir in tomato puree and spices.
4. Add paneer cubes.
5. Mix in cream and garam masala.
6. Garnish with coriander.
